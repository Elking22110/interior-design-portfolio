Moataz Gabr established in 2008 originally In Hurghada & Asyut, Masr Al Gedida and now in Shorook City and assuitv and hurgada. The Company is owned by Engineer Moataz Gabr, Specialized in Interior & Architecture Design Services. Our Area of Expertise: Interior Design Services, Architecture, Landscape Design, Execution & Site Supervision, Kitchen Design, Dressings, Bathroom Units. We provide you with the best service, to be in tune with your requirements and to anticipate your needs. Our reputation is based on the fundamental virtues of competence, coherence and reliability, and we provide a high level of expertise and state-of-the-art turnkey solutions that radically change the way you view a workspace. Our strength is that we offer new designs and materials that are not well known on the market to distinguish each design and accomplish a unique one
